About ATC 05 Set Old Boys Association

“Stronger Together — Celebrating Brotherhood and Growth”

Who We Are

The ATC 05 Set Old Boys Association is a registered body made up of proud 2005 graduates of Assumpta Technical College. Born out of friendship and shared memories, we exist to maintain strong bonds among members while giving back to our alma mater and community.

Our Vision

To build a united, progressive, and impactful community of ATC 05 Set members — fostering brotherhood, promoting growth, and contributing positively to society and our alma mater.

Our Mission

  • To strengthen the bond of friendship and unity among members.
  • To promote the welfare and development of all members.
  • To support our alma mater through meaningful projects and mentorship.
  • To uphold excellence, integrity, and service in all we do.

Our Core Values

  • Unity: We stand together as one family, always supporting each other.
  • Integrity: We maintain honesty, transparency, and respect in all our dealings.
  • Service: We are committed to making a positive impact in our community and school.
  • Excellence: We strive to be the best in all we do.
