Our Mission & Vision

What we stand for and where we're headed as a community.

Our Mission

  • Strengthen bonds of friendship and unity through transparent engagement and mutual support
  • Promote member welfare and development via collaboration and empowerment platforms
  • Support our alma mater with meaningful projects, mentorship, and partnerships
  • Uphold excellence, integrity, and service—the foundation of the ATC spirit
Join Membership View Events →

Our Vision

To build a united, progressive, and impactful community of ATC 05 Set alumni—fostering deep brotherhood, promoting personal and collective growth, and contributing meaningfully to society and our alma mater.

We envision a legacy of continuous impact, where every member thrives and our collective efforts create lasting positive change.
