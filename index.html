Our Purpose

What drives ATC 05SET — clear aims that guide our programs and service.

Our Mission

  • To strengthen the bond of friendship and unity among members through regular engagement, transparency, and mutual support.
  • To promote the welfare and development of all members, offering a platform for collaboration, assistance, and empowerment.
  • To support and contribute to the growth of our alma mater through meaningful projects, mentorship, and partnership initiatives.
  • To uphold the values of excellence, integrity, and service that define the ATC spirit.

    • See upcoming initiatives →

    Vision Statement

    To build a united, progressive, and impactful community of ATC 05 Set members — fostering brotherhood, promoting personal and collective growth, and contributing positively to society and our alma mater.
